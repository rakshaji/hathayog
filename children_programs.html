Children's Programs

Isha Hatha Yoga for Children introduces yoga through simple, life enhancing asanas and offers a unique possibility for every child to experience a joyful blossoming of their natural potential.



We offer both online and offline classes for kids. Online classes can be found here.



Benefits:
  • Enhances concentration and memory, focus, and mind/body coordination.
  • Helps in preventing obesity, asthma, sinusitis and other chronic ailments.
  • Leads to wellbeing and the proper development of body and mind.
