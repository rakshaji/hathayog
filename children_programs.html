Thoppukarnam

Brain Yoga

Sadilaja

Lubricating The Joints

One part of UPA yoga is called sadilaja. Sadilaja means born out of looseness. It is loosening not only the muscles and the skeletal system, it is also about loosening the energy system.

This program offers a simple upa yoga practices which are powerful set of 10 practices that activate the joints, muscles and energy system, bringing ease to the whole system. Based on a sophisticated understanding of the body’s mechanics, Upa Yoga dispels inertia in the body’s energy and brings ease to the whole system.

Age - 7 yrs & above

Relieves physical stress and tiredness

Strengthens the joints and muscles

Rejuvenates the body after periods of inactivity

Negates the effects of jetlag and long travel
