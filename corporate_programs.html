Corporate Programs

At HathaYog, we cater to the health and wellbeing requirements of individuals, organizations, and institutions with our “Wellness modules”, customized for the corporate workforce and business leaders.

You would appreciate that organizations are rapidly incorporating wellness initiatives such as stress and anxiety management, fitness programs and holistic health programs to produce motivated, stress-free and productive workforce. Physiological and psychological wellbeing of an individual ​can ​drive excellence and ​increased ​productivity in organizations. Towards this objective, we at impart Classical Hatha Yoga practices tailored to cater to the needs of contemporary executive​s​.

The programs will be conducted by teachers trained ​and certified by ​Isha Hatha School of Yoga, ​Coimbatore​, Tamil Nadu, India​. Teachers at HathaYog ​have undergone over 1750 hrs of intensive training and ​are certified to teach over 130 different modules to a variety of audiences at teaching these Yoga practices​.

HathaYog has been collaborating with corporates ​and organizations ​to offer regular in-house Hatha Yoga classes for employees and have witnessed ​significance changes in the employee-health​ and several positive impacts in workplace.

Some of​ the benefits of these modules include:

  • Relieves stress and tiredness
  • Increases mental alertness and focus
  • Increases endurance and energy levels
  • Relief of chronic health conditions
  • Healthy and joyful individuals
  • Less Burn on
  • Increased productivity & effectiveness
