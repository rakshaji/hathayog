Why learn from us

Classical Hatha Yoga

Realised Master

These practices have been selected and designed by Sadhguru to develop mental clarity and focus, boost vigour and vitality, balance hormonal levels and take years off the body, bringing a sense of lightness and freedom.

Purity

An unparalleled opportunity to learn practices derived from a yogic tradition maintained in its full purity and vibrancy for thousands of years.

Holistic

The practices are not taught merely for physical fitness and strength. They are a holistic process of naturally achieving a certain mastery over the body and mind, so as to come to a state of health, joy, and bliss.

Lifetime

Once you complete a program you will have a practice for life. There is no need to attend weekly classes or purchase special equipment.

Highly Trained

The classes will be conducted by highly trained teachers who have undergone a minimum of 1750+ hours of intense training at the Isha Yoga Centre in India.

Support

There is ongoing support and practice sessions available to further refine and deepen your sadhana, enabling a person in the most fundamental way, to flower into their ultimate potential.

Surya Kriya

Traditionally available only to select groups of yogis, Surya Kriya is a comprehensive spiritual practice that is ideal for the hectic pace of today's world.

Surya Shakti

Life on the planet is solar powered, including human system. Surya Shakti is a way of using that energy. It does wonders to your physical and mental states.

Surya Shakti For Kids

Children will learn Surya Shakti, an active form of the Sun Salutation composed of 18 powerful postures. It is designed as a holistic process for physical fitness.

Yogasanas

Yogasanas are a way of aligning in the inner system and adjusting it to the celestial geometry, becoming in sync with the existence. Relief of chronic health conditions

Angamardana

Angamardana, a fitness system rooted in yoga, offers everyone the opportunity to invigorate the body and reach peak physical and mental health. It uses your own body weight and needs no equipment.

Bhuta Shudhi

Bhuta Shuddhi means “purification of the five elements” which gets to the root of imbalances in the body that lead to disease. This simple practice reorganizes your system on the elemental level.

Jal Neti

Keeping your sinuses well-balanced and maintaining free movement within them is very important. Jala Neti is towards this – it reduces the mucus in the system. But this practice has to be properly imparted.

Nada Yoga

Nada Yoga uses the power of sound to activate a particular kind of energy in different parts of the body, bringing a lasting sense of inner pleasantness. It helps in bringing resolve in life.

Meditation

Everything that human beings have created on this planet was essentially first created in our minds. So, these meditations will help you to manifest love, health, peace and success in your life.

Bhakti Sadhana

Bhakti Sadhana is a very simple, very wonderful way of doing things. Bhakti literally means devotion. Nobody experiences this world with as much pleasantness as a devotee does.

Thoppukarnam (Brain Yoga)

The word comes from the Sanskrit words 'thorpi' meaning 'holding by hands' and 'karnam' means 'ears'. With usage it has become 'Thoppukarnam'. It activates the brain.
