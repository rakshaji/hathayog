Shanmukhi

Shanmukhi mudra is a simple but subtle practice that brightens and rejuvenates the face and eyes and brings about a state of balance leading toward increased awareness and meditativeness.

Age limit - 14 years and above

Benefits

Brightens the eyes and face. It improves the aura in the face. People concerned about their beauty can do it, actors can do it.

Improves vision

Helps turn a person inward, prepares one for pratyahara. Enhances awareness

Rejuvenates different parts of the face, including helping with ailments related to nose ears, eyes.

Helps relieve vertigo and tinnitus

Brings about mental balance
